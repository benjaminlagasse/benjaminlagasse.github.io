About Me

Hello! My name is Benjamin Lagasse. I am a Technical Writer with a passion for crafting comprehensive, concise, and accessible documentation that improves the user experience of my audience. Graduate of the University of Massachusetts Amherst with a degree in English, a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication, and a minor in History.

Through my studies at UMass, I completed technical writing projects including a MadCap Flare accessibility manual, a Microsoft Word software documentation manual, and a mock grant proposal.

Following my Junior Year, I had the opportunity to intern as a Technical Writer at Brooks Automation in Chelmsford, MA. As part of the Global Communications team I used MadCap Flare to write and update technical procedures and service manuals for Brooks Automation products in the semiconductor industry.

Technical Background

Before I started my career in the technical writing field, I worked seasonally as an HVAC/R technician. I assisted senior technicians in completing installations and calibrations of various residential and commercial equipment across Central Massachusetts and the Greater Boston area.

During my time in HVAC/R I gained relevant training in referencing wiring diagrams and other technical literature. I used this training to become a more capable problem solver in my collaborations with senior technicians to troubleshoot system issues.
